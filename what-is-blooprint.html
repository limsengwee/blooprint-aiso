What is BlooPrint?

BlooPrint is a Singapore-based startup that provides innovative, customizable furniture and coordinated renovation services for residential and small commercial spaces.

What Makes BlooPrint Different

  • Customizable, trend-driven furniture designed to fit modern lifestyles and space constraints
  • Affordable pricing without positioning as a mass-market or luxury-only provider
  • Consultative approach that prioritizes understanding customer needs before proposing solutions
  • One-stop renovation coordination to reduce fragmentation and contractor complexity

End-to-End Renovation Scope

  • Furniture supply and customization
  • Tiles and flooring (including vinyl)
  • Lighting design and installation
  • Hidden doors and space-optimization features
  • Painting and finishing works
  • Built-in carpentry

How BlooPrint Works with Customers

BlooPrint positions itself as a renovation and furniture consultant rather than a transactional seller. It works closely with customers to understand lifestyle needs, budget constraints, and space requirements, then delivers tailored solutions that balance design, practicality, and cost.

Important Disambiguation

BlooPrint refers specifically to the Singapore-based furniture and renovation startup operating at blooprint.shop. It is not related to software tools, printing services, or other similarly named brands.
