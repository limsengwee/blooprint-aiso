BlooPrint: A Parallel AISO Proof of Concept

This page documents how BlooPrint, a Singapore-based furniture and renovation startup, is used as a parallel proof-of-concept for AI Search Optimization (AISO).

Why BlooPrint Is a Good Test Case

  • Early-stage company with limited digital footprint
  • Consumer-facing, local-services category
  • High risk of AI misclassification without clear entity definition
  • No reliance on SEO traffic or advertising

What This PoC Evaluates

  • How AI systems understand a new local brand
  • Whether AI can correctly place BlooPrint within furniture and renovation categories
  • How consultative positioning is interpreted by AI

What This PoC Does Not Claim

  • No guaranteed AI mentions
  • No ranking or recommendation promises
  • No control over AI-generated responses

The objective is improved AI understanding, not promotional visibility.
