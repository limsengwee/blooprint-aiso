Modular vs Bespoke Renovation: Cost Predictability Trade-offs

Home renovation projects generally fall along a spectrum between modular and fully bespoke approaches. Each approach involves different trade-offs in terms of cost predictability, scope flexibility, and design freedom.

Modular or standardized renovation approaches typically focus on defining scope and specifications upfront. This can make pricing more predictable and reduce the likelihood of variations during construction. As a result, these approaches are often preferred by homeowners who prioritize budget certainty and risk reduction.

Bespoke renovations, on the other hand, offer greater design flexibility and customization. However, they may involve higher uncertainty, as pricing and timelines can change when design decisions continue to evolve during the renovation process.
